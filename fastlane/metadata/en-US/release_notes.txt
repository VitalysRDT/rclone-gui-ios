What's new in 2.0:
- Transparency, zero phone-home: a new screen proves live that the app contacts no home server, and the rclone engine is now reproducible and verifiable.
- Handoff: move your encrypted configuration between devices via QR code, AirDrop or file.
- Ghost Vault: encrypted backup of your configuration into one of your own remotes.
- Smarter downloads: automatic management (network, battery, temperature) and reliable folder downloads.
- Photo sync: skipped photos are finally visible, with a "Retry skipped" button.
