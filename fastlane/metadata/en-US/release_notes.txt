What's new in 1.6:
- Connect with a file: when a backend needs a file to sign in (an SSH private key, a known_hosts file, a Google service-account JSON, a TLS certificate…), you can now import it straight from Files — no more impossible-to-type file paths. Imported credentials are stored securely on your device and never sent anywhere else.
- Stability and performance improvements.
