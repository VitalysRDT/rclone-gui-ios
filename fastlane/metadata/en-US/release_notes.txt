What's new in 2.1:
- Edit a remote: a new "Manage remotes" screen lets you fix a setting or re-authorize an expired OAuth account without recreating everything — your existing passwords and tokens stay hidden.
- Remote Lens: peek at a remote file without downloading it — image preview with its EXIF data, and PDF first page.
- Deletion fixed: deleting a file or folder, or moving it to the trash, works again.
