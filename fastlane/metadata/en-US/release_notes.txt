What's new in 1.4:
- New clouds: Drime, Internxt and Filen join the 70+ supported services (Internxt and Filen are end-to-end encrypted).
- Easier connections: a new "where to get your credentials" panel guides setup for Pixeldrain, 1Fichier, ImageKit, Internet Archive, Gofile, Storj, NetStorage and more.
- Simpler composite remotes: a picker lets you choose the underlying storage (and folder) for alias, union, combine, and more — no more typing "remote:path" by hand.
- Reliability: fixed connecting to password-protected remotes (SFTP, FTP, WebDAV, SMB…).
- Locked remotes are now hidden from Recents and Favorites.
- Translation and folder-summary display improvements.
