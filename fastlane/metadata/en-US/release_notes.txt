What's new in 1.5:
- Built-in video player: play your videos right inside the app — including MKV, AVI, WebM and TS — thanks to a multi-format engine. Subtitles (embedded and sidecar files), audio tracks, resume where you left off, and background audio. Plus your choice of playing in-app or in an external app (Infuse, VLC).
- Gallery: a new grid view with thumbnails for your photos and videos. Switch between list and grid in any folder, or turn on "Media only" for a true gallery experience. A thumbnail generation setting (Wi-Fi only by default) keeps you in control of your data.
- Privacy: a new option to exclude the app's data from iCloud backups.
- Stability and performance improvements.

Thanks for using Rclone GUI! We'd love your feedback.
